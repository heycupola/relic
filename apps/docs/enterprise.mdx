Relic Enterprise gives your organization a dedicated secrets infrastructure, fully managed or self-hosted, with direct support from our team.

## Deployment Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cloud (Managed)" icon="cloud">
    We host and manage a dedicated Relic instance for your organization. You get an isolated environment with no shared infrastructure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cloud (Your Infrastructure)" icon="server">
    Relic runs on your own cloud provider (AWS, GCP, Azure). You control the infrastructure, we help with setup and updates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Self-Hosted" icon="building">
    Run Relic entirely on your own hardware or private network. Full control over data residency and network policies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hybrid" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left">
    Mix and match. Keep secrets on-premise while using our cloud for authentication and key management, or the other way around.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What You Get

- **Dedicated instance** for your organization with isolated data
- **Unlimited projects and team members**
- **Custom retention and audit policies** to meet your compliance requirements
- **Priority support** via Slack with direct access to the engineering team
- **Onboarding assistance** for setup, migration, and team rollout
- **SLA guarantees** tailored to your needs
